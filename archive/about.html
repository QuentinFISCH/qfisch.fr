SERPOLLET

Electrician assistant - Temp work - 2 weeks Electricien assistant - Intérimaire - 2 semaines

From August 10 to 21, 2020, I carried out an interim assignment for two weeks as an electrician assistant, having no qualification in this field.

This mission allowed me to learn a lot about the electrician trade, the way a small building site is managed, as well as many things in electricity. In particular, I was able to better understand how the public lighting network of a municipality works, how to apprehend a failure and find a solution, and above all to learn the steps to replace the entire public lighting system with more modern lighting.

Indeed, the site on which we worked during these two weeks aimed to replace all the public lighting of a municipality with more recent LED lighting. To do so, I was first able to participate in the laying and pulling of new cables in order to be able to supply the new lighting, then to put the new lamps on the existing masts.

This mission did not improve my skills in the IT field, but it allowed me to leave this environment and discover new horizons. Du 10 au 21 août 2020, j'ai réalisé une mission d'intérim pendant deux semaines en tant qu'assistant électricien, n'ayant pas de qualification dans ce domaine.

Cette mission m'a permis d'apprendre beaucoup sur le métier d'électricien, la façon dont un petit chantier est géré, ainsi que de nombreuses choses en électricité. J'ai notamment pu mieux comprendre comment fonctionne le réseau d'éclairage public d'une commune, comment appréhender une panne et trouver une solution, et surtout apprendre les étapes pour remplacer tout le système d'éclairage public vers un éclairage plus moderne.

En effet, le chantier sur lequel nous avons travaillé durant ces deux semaines avait pour objectif de remplacer tous les luminaires publics d'une commune par un éclairage à LED plus récent. Pour se faire, j'ai d'abord pu participer à la pose et au tirage de nouveaux câbles afin de pouvoir alimenter le nouvel éclairage, pour ensuite poser les nouvelles lampes sur les mâts existants.

Cette mission ne pas permis d'améliorer mes compétences dans le domaine informatique, mais elle m'a permis de sortir de ce milieu et de découvrir de nouveaux horizons.

SPEAT

WEBMASTER - Intership - 1 month WEBMASTER - Stage - 1 mois

From June 29 to July 31, 2020, I had the opportunity to work as an intern in a small Start-up, called EasTechnology.

This internship being my first experience in IT, I learned a lot about the functioning of such a group and the necessary organization. In addition, this internship was carried out by telework, which gives a new dimension to this special experience.

Associated with the web development of the company based on the development of a mobile application (SPEAT, of which I am not allowed to reveal the subject), I discovered the functioning of WordPress, which to me. At first seemed much more complex than the classic HTML and CSS, but which I was able to get used to quickly. I made a large part of the current website, while following the guidelines set by the company.

Subsequently, I was able to work on a very interesting part of web development. Indeed, I produced two pages of statistics allowing customers who have subscribed to a certain program to have a better vision of their business thanks to precise data. These statistics that I was able to display were retrieved from information from a Firebase database, which I had the opportunity to discover its downs.

This internship allowed me to improve my skills in JavaScript, PHP, HTML / CSS and to discover Firebase and WordPress. Du 29 juin au 31 juillet 2020, j'ai eu l'occasion de travailler en tant que stagiaire au sein d'une petite Start-up, dénommée EasTechnology.
